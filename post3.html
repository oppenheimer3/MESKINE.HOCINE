AMC operating frequencies prediction

Summary

the Artificial Magnetic Conductor is an artificial material that has many applications in the electromagnetic domain, one of which is the ability to enhance the performance of antennas by increasing its gain and directivity. However this property is not present in all the frequency spectrum, for example a single-band AMC can operate only within 3one frequency. These frequencies depends on the design and dimensions of the structure.

In my final year project I was given a triple-band AMC and was asked to build a mathematical model that determine these frequencies, after finishing my project I had the idea that building a machine learning model will make the job easier. so the goal of this project was to build a machine learning model that predict the operating frequencies of the AMC based on its dimensions.
